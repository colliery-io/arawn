## Status Updates **[REQUIRED]**

### 2026-03-22: Completed
- **WsConnectionTracker leak fix**: Added cleanup of empty timestamp vectors in `check_rate()`. When an IP's timestamps all expire, the entry is removed from the HashMap. Also triggers a periodic `retain()` purge of all stale IPs to prevent unbounded growth.
- **Hardcoded URLs**: Investigated — the `http://localhost:8080` default in `resolve_server_url()` matches `ServerConfig::default()` bind address. It's a reasonable fallback, not a hardcoded bug. Other occurrences are in tests and documentation comments.
- All 6 WS connection tests pass
